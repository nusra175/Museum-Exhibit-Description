History of the Internet

Museum Exhibit Description

About the Exhibit

This exhibit explores the development of the Internet from its early beginnings to the global network we use today. Visitors will discover the technologies, people, and milestones that shaped the digital world.

Key Sections

ARPANET

The first packet-switching network and the foundation of the Internet.

Birth of the Web

Tim Berners-Lee introduced the World Wide Web in 1989, making the Internet accessible to everyone.

Modern Internet

Explore how social media, streaming, and cloud computing transformed the digital landscape.
