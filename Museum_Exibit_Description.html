History of the Internet Exhibit

Explore the fascinating journey from ARPANET to the World Wide Web

About the Exhibit

Join us for an interactive museum experience tracing the evolution of the Internet. Discover how early networks connected researchers, the birth of email, the rise of the web, and the impact of social media today.

Exhibit Highlights

1960s - ARPANET

The first computer network designed to share information between research labs and universities.

1970s-80s - Email & Protocols

Learn about the creation of email, TCP/IP protocols, and the foundations of modern networking.

1990s - The World Wide Web

Experience the launch of the web and the first browsers that brought the Internet to the public.

2000s-Present - Social Media & Connectivity

See how social media, streaming, and mobile Internet transformed communication worldwide.
